Climate change is an urgent issue that requires an immediate and widespread response, yet communication that effectively spurs people into action remains a challenge.

Despite decades of effort, traditional approaches to climate communication often struggle to resonate with audiences, meaning there remains a significant gap between what we know needs to be done and how much progress we’ve actually made.

Communicating Climate Change is dedicated to helping you do exactly that.

Communicating Climate Change exists to help bridge this gap. By integrating a wealth of knowledge from across disciplines and worldviews into our collective climate communications competence, we aim help you engage, empower, and activate audiences, driving immediate, inspired, and impactful climate action.

For Creatives for Climate

Host of Communicating Climate Change, Dickon Bonvik-Stone, created a climate communications learning adventure for Creatives for Climate, a network of 40,000 purpose-driven professionals working within marketing, advertising, PR ,and beyond.

Bringing together insights from across academia, industry, and the experience of a kaleidoscope of experts, the self-paced course is designed to help others develop their climate communications skills.

For UN Climate Change

Host of Communicating Climate Change, Dickon Bonvik-Stone, was invited to be a panelist at this webinar from UN Climate Change.
